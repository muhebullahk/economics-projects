Research

Working papers, ongoing projects, and research agenda

Research Areas

Primary fields of scholarly inquiry

Applied Microeconomics

Empirical analysis of markets, individual decision-making under constraints, and efficiency of resource allocation.

Labour Economics

Wage formation, employment dynamics, returns to education, and the effects of labour market policies on worker outcomes.

Public Economics & Policy

Program evaluation of government interventions, optimal taxation, social insurance design, and distributional consequences of policy.

Development Economics

Institutional determinants of economic growth, poverty dynamics, and micro-level responses to policy in developing economies.

Econometric Methods

Causal inference, quasi-experimental design, panel data econometrics, and machine learning applications in economics.

Health & Human Capital

Returns to education and health investments, healthcare market analysis, and insurance incentive effects.
