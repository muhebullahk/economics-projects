Research

Working papers, ongoing projects, and research agenda

Research Areas

Primary fields of scholarly inquiry

Macroeconomics

Business cycle dynamics, aggregate fluctuations, and the structural forces driving economic expansions and recessions.

Housing Markets

Housing investment over the business cycle, the macroeconomic consequences of housing booms and busts, and pandemic-era housing dynamics.

Monetary & International Economics

International monetary economics, open-economy macroeconomics, and monetary policy in the Euro Area and other currency unions.

Labour Market Dynamics

Employment and wage dynamics in the construction sector, and the relationship between housing cycles and labour market outcomes.

DSGE Modelling

Bayesian estimation of dynamic stochastic general equilibrium models, learning efficiency shocks, and knowledge capital in business cycle models.

Computational Methods

MATLAB, DYNARE, and Bayesian methods for structural macroeconomic estimation and simulation.
