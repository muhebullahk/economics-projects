ECON 204 – Contemporary Economic Issues

This course is designed to introduce students to contemporary economics issues, with a particular emphasis on the Canadian and U.S. economies. Topics covered include, but are not limited to, labour market dynamics, income inequality, economic growth, globalization, and international trade. The course will also explore the theory and practice of monetary and fiscal policies in Canada, examining how these policies impact economic stability, growth, and social welfare. Students will gain an understanding of the key economic challenges that Canada is facing today and will develop analytical tools to assess these issues from both a theoretical and practical perspective.
